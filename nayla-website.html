Why Nayla exists

STEAM education shouldn't depend on where you live.

We bring real, hands-on science, tech, and problem-solving programs to students in Aswan — taught by people from here, built for here.

Hands-on, not theoretical

Every program ends in something built — a project, a piece of code, a presented idea — not just a certificate.

Taught by Aswan's own

Our facilitators are local engineers and educators who understand the language, the context, and the students.

Open to every student

Nayla is for any student in Aswan and Upper Egypt aged 12–19 with curiosity and the will to show up.

Built for every student in Aswan.

Nayla started as a space for girls in STEAM — and that founding spirit still shapes how we teach. Today, Nayla is open to every student in Aswan and Upper Egypt, aged 12 to 19, who wants to learn, build, and grow.

Whoever you are, if you're curious and ready to show up, there's a seat for you in our next cohort.

Nayla recently completed its rebrand: from a girls-only initiative to a STEAM program open to all students in Aswan and Upper Egypt.
